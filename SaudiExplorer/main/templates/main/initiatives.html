🌍 Sustainability

Saudi Green Initiative

Launched in 2021, this national strategy aims to plant 10 billion trees, reduce carbon emissions, and protect 30% of land and marine areas by 2030. It is a key pillar in achieving environmental sustainability and a green economy.

🌳 Environment

Afforestation Projects

Saudi Arabia is working to plant billions of trees to combat desertification and expand green spaces. Around 600 million trees are expected to be planted by 2030.

💧 Innovation

Hydroponic Farming

Advanced hydroponic systems allow crops to grow without soil while reducing water consumption by up to 90%. These technologies support year-round production and efficient resource use.

💧 Sustainability

Water Conservation Initiative

Modern irrigation systems and policies are used to reduce water usage in agriculture, including limiting high-consumption crops and promoting efficient irrigation techniques.

👩‍🌾 Support

Farmer Support Programs

The government provides financial and technical support to farmers through subsidies and digital platforms like "Murshidak", offering expert agricultural advice and guidance.

📈 Investment

Agricultural Investment

Platforms like "Furas" offer agricultural investment opportunities, encouraging private sector participation in projects such as nurseries and fertilizer production.

🍽️ Security

Food Security Strategy

Saudi Arabia invests in agricultural projects locally and abroad to ensure stable food supply, reduce dependency on imports, and strengthen national food security.

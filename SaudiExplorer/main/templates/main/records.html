Agricultural Records

Discover Saudi Arabia’s remarkable agricultural achievements and world records.

🫒 Record

Al-Jouf — Largest Olive Farm

Al-Jouf is home to the largest olive farm in the world, with millions of olive trees producing high-quality olive oil and positioning Saudi Arabia as a global leader in olive production.

🌍 World Record
🌱 Sustainability

Asir — Largest Sustainable Farm

The Asir region is recognized for hosting one of the world’s largest sustainable agricultural projects, combining eco-friendly practices with modern farming techniques.

♻️ Global Impact
☕ Agriculture

Al-Baha — Coffee Capital

Al-Baha is considered the largest coffee-producing region in the Middle East, with growing investments in Saudi coffee cultivation and heritage farming.

🌍 Middle East Record
🍊 Agriculture

Najran — Citrus Production Hub

Najran hosts one of the largest citrus farming clusters in the world, producing oranges, lemons, and other citrus fruits at large scale.

🌍 World Scale
🌴 Heritage

Al-Ahsa — Largest Palm Oasis

Al-Ahsa Oasis is the largest palm oasis in the world, home to millions of date palms and recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

🏛️ UNESCO Site
🌹 Achievement

Taif — Rose Achievements

Taif roses have achieved global recognition, including a Guinness World Record in 2022 for the largest flower basket (84,450 roses). The annual festival attracts over 100,000 visitors daily, and more than 960 million roses are produced annually across 2,000 farms.

🏆 Guinness World Record

About Saudi Agriculture

A Land of Surprising Abundance

Despite its arid landscape, Saudi Arabia has cultivated a thriving agricultural sector shaped by centuries of ingenuity and modern innovation. The Kingdom is one of the world's largest producers of dates, and its farms yield wheat, vegetables, fruits, and aromatic herbs through advanced irrigation systems and pioneering agricultural technologies aligned with Vision 2030.
